Welcome To Hogwarts

Dear Student,
We are pleased to inform you that you have been accepted into Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry for the upcoming academic year. Enclosed with this letter, you will find a list of required books and equipment for your first year at Hogwarts. The Hogwarts Express will depart from Platform 9¾ at King's Cross Station at 11 a.m. Upon your arrival at Hogwarts, you will be sorted into one of our four houses—Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, or Slytherin. We look forward to welcoming you to Hogwarts and witnessing the incredible journey that lies ahead for you. Prepare for a year filled with magical discoveries, friendship, and academic excellence.
Yours sincerely,
Albus Dumbledore
Headmaster
Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry
